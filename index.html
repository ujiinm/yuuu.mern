Ingredients:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the dry ingredients: flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, cream together the butter and sugars until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs and vanilla extract to the wet mixture and beat well.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown around the edges.
  9. Allow cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
